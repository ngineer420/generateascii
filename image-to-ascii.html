About the image-to-ASCII converter

This tool turns any image or photo into ASCII art by sampling it onto a character grid and mapping each cell's brightness to a character — a technique called luminance mapping. Drop in a JPG, PNG, GIF, or WebP (or paste one from your clipboard), then dial in the width, character set, brightness, contrast, and gamma until it looks right. Choose monochrome or full color, and a dark or light background. All of it runs on a canvas inside your browser, so your images never leave your device.

Copy the result to your clipboard, or export it as a .txt file or a rendered .png image. Want to make a banner from text instead? Try the text to ASCII generator.
